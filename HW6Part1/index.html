3 Final Fantasy XIV Character Cards

Ser Aymeric

Age: 32

Height: 6' 7.5"

Race: Elezen

Ser Aymeric is the bastard son of the previous ruler of the citystate of Ishgard Archduke Thordan After helping to end the Dragonsong War with the Warrior of Light he was selected to help enter the city into a new era.

Zenos yae Galvus

Age: 26

Height: 8'

Race: Garlean

Zenos was a child prodigy and advanced quickly through the Garlean military. Quickly growing bored of everyone around him, he found that the only thing that brought him any pleasure was the hunt. Either beast or man, it did not matter as long as it gave him the joy he craved.

Bahamut

Age: 5,000

Height: 267 miles

Race: Dragon/ Primal

The original dragon Bahamut had been killed by the Allagan Empire. Enraged, his mate Tiamat gathered many other dragons to summon the spirit of Bahamut. This summoned primal being was not the same as the original but a ravenous beast. The Allagans sealed him inside a false moon with his servants, where he remained for millenia gathering power.

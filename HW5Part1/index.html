Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a nonstick pan. Fry potatoes until crispy. Add onions and fry until translucent. Drain oil from pan.
  2. Return potatoes and onions to pan, arranging in single layer. reduce heat to low.
  3. Mix eggs with salt an peper to taste. Pour eggs over vegetables, taking care to mix evenly.
  4. Cover the eggs with a large plate and flip the omlette onto the plate. Slide the omlette back onto the pan.
  5. Continue to cook until cooked through.
  6. Remove from the pan in the same way as before, slice, and enjoy!
